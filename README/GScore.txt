## **Dice Game**

### **Description**
A **fun and interactive dice game** where players roll two dice and try to win by hitting a lucky number.

### **Features**
- Rolls two dice with randomized outcomes.
- Tracks wins and losses.
- Fun messages and emojis for player engagement.

### **Installation**
1. **Compile the program:**
   ```sh
   javac DiceGame.java
   ```
2. **Run the program:**
   ```sh
   java DiceGame
   ```

### **Usage**
- Press Enter to roll the dice.
- Rolling a 7 or 11 wins, while 2, 3, or 12 loses.
- Play again or exit.
